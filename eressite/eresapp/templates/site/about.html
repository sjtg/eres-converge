ERES CONVERGE IRB

ERES Converge is the only private IRB currently registered in Zambia. It is registered with the Office of Register of Companies in Lusaka Zambia.

Do they follow national/international guidelines?

ERES Converge IRB is accredited to the Office for Protection of Human Research Participants (OHRP) in the United States of America and so have an IRB number and a Federal Wide Assurance (FWA) number. As indicated above ERES Converge is registered as a Limited Company and has a Board of Directors. It is locally accredited to the National Health Research Ethics Committee in the Zambia’s Ministry of Health which accredits all Research Ethics Committees and or IRBs in the country. The committee adheres to regulations as outlined in the Declaration of Helsinki, the Belmont Report, CIOMS and the International Guidelines for Biomedical Research. ERES Converge adheres strictly to International Standards of Good Clinical Practice.
